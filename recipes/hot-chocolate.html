Hot chocolate

Desctiption

To top it all off, a good drink is necessary during winter. That's why we are offering a recipe for hot chocolate. There are many styles, but this is the one which isn't extremely complicated but it tastes amazing.

Some might even dip their food into the hot chocolate, such as pastries. What I don't find appealing at all is marshmallows; they just ruin the drink by sweetening it too much. If you want to add sugar to make it sweet, go on. But, bear in mind that the chocolate has already got some sugar.

Ingredients

  • 200 ml of milk
  • 30 to 50 gr of chocolate
  • (Optional) sugar
  • (Optional) anise, cinnamon and any other spice you like

Steps

  1. Chop down the chocolate into small pieces.
  2. Throw the ingredients except for the milk into a pot, then cover with milk, but use as little milk as possible. Save the rest for later.
  3. Cook the hot chocolate in extremely low heat for about 15 mintues. As low as possible. Stir occasionally.
  4. Let it cool down for a few minutes, then strain it and enjoy!!

Tips and warnings

  • How to choose the best chocolate: there are two main rules that you have to follow to make a great hot chocolate. These are:
    1. The fewer the ingredients, the better
    2. The darker, the better
    Using white chocolate is a sin and it deserves hate.
  • While cooking, the milk might evaporate. In that case, immediately turn off the heat, or else the milk is going to overflow and it's going to be a mess. After that, stir the hot chocolate slowly for around a minute, and turn on the heat once again. Don't stop the timer.
  • If you want more creaminess, you can do one of two things: either use less milk or cook it for a longer time.
